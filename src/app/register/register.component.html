  • About

    As technology advances rapidly, it is essential to understand the impact on healthcare. The integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Robotics has the potential to transform the delivery of healthcare services and enhance patient outcomes. LLMs excel in natural language processing and have various applications in healthcare, such as medical coding and clinical decision support. Robotics can bring advanced intelligence capabilities directly to patients, improving the care experience. The collection of care delivery information through these technologies can revolutionize diagnosis and treatment. This blog will delve into the integration of these technologies, examining the challenges and opportunities they present and discussing solutions to overcome them.
